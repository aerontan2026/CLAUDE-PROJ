## Design Trends
- **GenUI & Liquid Layouts Dominate** — Interfaces are no longer static. AI now assembles components in real-time based on context—buttons reorganize, forms appear/disappear, and layouts adapt instantly to user intent. This "liquid layout" approach marks a fundamental shift from fixed screens. [MotionGility](https://motiongility.com/future-of-ui-ux-design/)

- **Trust & Explainability Are Design Challenges** — In 2026, the biggest design problem for AI experiences is building user confidence through transparency, control, consistency, and graceful failure. Leading products now display reasoning upfront and allow users to intervene when AI makes mistakes. [Nielsen Norman Group](https://www.nngroup.com/articles/state-of-ux-2026/)

- **Designer Role Evolves to "Design System Governor"** — Modern UX designers are becoming strategic conductors, defining strict atomic components and rules that AI will manipulate. The role now spans psychology, data analysis, and technical knowledge—not just wireframes. [UX Design](https://uxdesign.cc/10-ux-design-shifts-you-cant-ignore-in-2026-8f0da1c6741d?gi=a5949a78673d)
