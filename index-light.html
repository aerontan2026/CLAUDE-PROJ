Our Six-Stage Process

How We Deliver a Permanent Fix

Every project follows a rigorous, documented methodology — from first visit to final handover.

STAGE 01

Site Assessment & Diagnosis

Thorough on-site inspection to identify leak sources, moisture pathways, and structural contributing factors.

STAGE 02

System Recommendation

Tailored system selection matched to your substrate, exposure level, and building occupancy requirements.

STAGE 03

Surface Preparation

Concrete repairs, substrate priming, crack injection, and surface profiling before membrane application.

STAGE 04

Membrane Application

Precision membrane application with full attention to terminations, upstands, penetrations, and junctions.

STAGE 05

Repair & Reinstatement

Coordinated repair of related elements including tiles, linings, and ceiling reinstatement works.

STAGE 06

Checks & Handover Docs

Water testing, visual inspections, and a full documentation package for your building records.
