    Leak Problems We Solve

    Every Water Ingress Issue
    Has a Root Cause

    We don't apply band-aid fixes. We trace the source, understand the failure mechanism, and apply the right system the first time.

    Ceiling & Wall Seepage

    Water staining, dampness, and blistering paint traced back to slab failures, joint breakdowns, or hidden pipe leaks.

    Roof Leakage

    Ponding water, failed membrane laps, open upstands, and corroded flashings causing recurring interior damage.

    Bathroom Water Ingress

    Grout failure, missing waterproof membrane, failed floor traps — allowing water to penetrate into the unit below.

    Crack & Joint Failures

    Structural and movement cracks that act as direct water pathways — requiring crack injection or flexible sealant systems.

    Facade Leakage

    Wind-driven rain penetration through external wall cladding, window reveals, expansion joints, and coping details.

    Interior Damage from Leaks

    Secondary damage to ceilings, floors, walls, and finishes caused by prolonged, unresolved water ingress issues.

    Our Process

    Our Waterproofing Process

    A structured, methodology-driven approach — from the first inspection to final handover with zero shortcuts.

    01
    Site Inspection
    Visual survey, moisture readings, water testing, and documentation of all affected areas.
    02
    Root-Cause Diagnosis
    Identify the primary failure mechanism — not just where the water exits, but where it enters.
    03
    System Recommendation
    We specify the correct membrane, sealant, or injection system for the specific failure mode.
    04
    Rectification & Application
    Executed by trained in-house technicians following system manufacturer specifications.
    05
    Testing & Handover
    Flood test, hose test, or visual verification — then written warranty and documentation issued.
