Scope of Work

Our Six-Stage Process

A structured, end-to-end approach so nothing is missed — from first inspection to final handover.

Get a Quote
STAGE 01

Site Assessment & Diagnosis

Thorough on-site inspection to identify leak sources, moisture pathways, and structural contributing factors.

STAGE 02

System Recommendation

Tailored waterproofing system selection matched to your substrate, exposure level, and occupancy requirements.

STAGE 03

Surface Preparation & Rectification

Concrete repairs, substrate priming, crack injection, and surface profiling prior to membrane application.

STAGE 04

Membrane Application & Detailing

Precision membrane application with full attention to terminations, upstands, penetrations, and junctions.

STAGE 05

Repair & Reinstatement Scopes

Coordinated repair of related building elements including tiles, linings, and ceiling reinstatement.

STAGE 06

Post-Work Checks & Handover Docs

Water testing, visual inspections, and a full documentation package delivered for your building records.
